Unit Economics
Blended ARPU (Annual) — Weighted average across Starter / Professional / Enterprise mix
CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) — (Year 1 sales cost + marketing) ÷ new customers added in Year 1
LTV (Customer Lifetime Value) — ARPU × (1 ÷ annual churn rate)
LTV / CAC Ratio — Target >3× for a healthy SaaS business
Months to Recover CAC — CAC ÷ (ARPU ÷ 12)
Gross Margin per Customer — (ARPU/12 − AI cost − Infra cost) ÷ (ARPU/12)
